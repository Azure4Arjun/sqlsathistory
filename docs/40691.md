# SQLSaturday #441 - Denver 2015
Event Date: 19-09-2015 - Session time: 12:30:00 - Track: BI Information Delivery
## Speaker: Matt White
## Title: Light up your SQL investment with Pyramid Analytics
## Abstract:
### Come see how Pyramid Analytics delivers a powerful, business user friendly platform that empowers organizations to make the most of their Business Intelligence.  A demonstration will show you how to leverage your SQL environment to make Business Intelligence available to all within your organization.  See how all levels of users can access, understand and present the right information to the right people at the right time.
