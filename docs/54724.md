## Speaker: Aaron Bertrand
## Title: T-SQL : Bad Habits and Best Practices
## Abstract:
### Bad habits: we all have them. SELECT * is the obvious one; but in this session you will learn about various other habits and why they can be bad for performance or maintainability. You will also learn about best practices that will help you avoid falling into some of these bad habits.
