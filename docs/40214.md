# SQLSaturday #444 - Kansas City 2015
Event Date: 03-10-2015 - Session time: 14:30:00 - Track: Advanced Analysis Techniques
## Speaker: Myles Gartland
## Title: Trees, Forests and Predictive Models
## Abstract:
### This session will introduce people to a couple of modern forms of data mining for predictive analytics problems. We will review Decision Trees, Random Forests and other ensemble methods. These are methods are highly popular in data science. We will show some cases studies of how these are used in business for increased prediction .
